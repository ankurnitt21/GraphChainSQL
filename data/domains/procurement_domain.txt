# Warehouse Procurement & Supplier Domain Knowledge

## Purchase Order Lifecycle
POs track procurement from suppliers:
1. DRAFT → Being prepared, not yet sent
2. SUBMITTED → Sent to supplier
3. CONFIRMED → Supplier confirmed availability
4. RECEIVED → Goods arrived at warehouse
5. CANCELLED → PO cancelled

## Supplier Management
Suppliers are rated on performance:
- rating: 0.00 to 5.00 scale based on delivery, quality
- lead_time_days: Expected days from order to delivery
- Use supplier rating for vendor selection decisions

## Purchase Order Lines
Each PO has line items:
- quantity_ordered: What we requested
- quantity_received: What actually arrived
- Partial receipts allowed (received < ordered)

## Procurement Key Metrics
- Cost price vs Unit price: Margin analysis
- Lead time: Planning and buffer stock calculation
- Supplier rating: Quality and reliability assessment

## Key Procurement Queries
- "Show open purchase orders" - pending deliveries
- "Which suppliers have rating above 4?" - top performers
- "List overdue POs past expected_delivery" - delays
- "What's total spend with supplier X?" - vendor analysis
- "Show products from supplier Y" - supplier catalog

## Supplier Contact Information
Each supplier has:
- contact_name, email, phone for communication
- city, country for logistics planning
- code: Unique identifier like SUP-TECH

## Receiving Process
When goods arrive:
1. Match to purchase_order_line
2. Update quantity_received
3. Create inventory_transaction (type=RECEIPT)
4. Update inventory.quantity_on_hand
5. If fully received, update PO status to RECEIVED
