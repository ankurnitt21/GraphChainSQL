# Warehouse Inventory Domain Knowledge

## Inventory Management Overview
The inventory system tracks all products stored across warehouse locations. Key concepts:

1. **Quantity On Hand (QOH)**: Physical stock present at a location
2. **Quantity Reserved**: Stock allocated to pending orders but not yet picked
3. **Quantity Available**: QOH minus Reserved - what can be sold/promised

## Inventory Transactions
All inventory movements are tracked via inventory_transaction with types:
- RECEIPT: Stock coming in from purchase orders
- PICK: Stock picked for sales order fulfillment
- TRANSFER: Movement between locations within warehouse
- ADJUSTMENT: Manual corrections (damage, shrinkage, cycle count)
- RETURN: Customer returns added back to inventory

## Lot Tracking & Expiry
- Each inventory record can have a lot_number for traceability
- Perishable products track expiry_date for FIFO/FEFO picking
- lot_number is unique per product-location combination

## Reorder Logic
Products have automatic reorder settings:
- min_stock_level: Absolute minimum to maintain safety stock
- reorder_point: Trigger level for new purchase orders
- reorder_qty: Standard quantity to order when triggered

## Key Queries for Inventory
- "Show products below reorder point" - inventory needing replenishment
- "What's the available quantity for SKU X?" - availability check
- "List expiring inventory in next 30 days" - expiry management
- "Show stock movement history for product Y" - audit trail
- "Which locations have product Z?" - stock locator

## Location Hierarchy
warehouse → zone → location (aisle/rack/shelf/bin)
Each location has max_weight_kg capacity and is_occupied status.
