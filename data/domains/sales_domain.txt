# Warehouse Sales & Fulfillment Domain Knowledge

## Sales Order Lifecycle
Orders progress through status workflow:
1. PENDING → Initial order created
2. CONFIRMED → Payment/credit verified
3. PICKING → Warehouse staff picking items
4. SHIPPED → Handed to carrier
5. DELIVERED → Confirmed delivery
6. CANCELLED → Order cancelled

## Order Priority System
Priority affects pick sequence and shipping method:
- LOW: Standard processing, economy shipping
- NORMAL: Default priority, ground shipping
- HIGH: Expedited processing, express options
- URGENT: Immediate pick, next-day/same-day

## Customer Types
- RETAIL: Individual/small business, lower volumes
- WHOLESALE: Bulk buyers, volume discounts
- DISTRIBUTOR: Regional partners, highest volumes

## Shipment Tracking
Each sales order can have multiple shipments:
- shipment_number: Internal tracking ID
- carrier: UPS, FedEx, USPS, etc.
- tracking_number: Carrier's tracking ID
- Status: PENDING → IN_TRANSIT → DELIVERED

## Key Sales Queries
- "Show pending orders by priority" - fulfillment queue
- "List orders for customer X" - customer history
- "What's the total sales this month?" - revenue tracking
- "Show shipped but undelivered orders" - in-transit monitoring
- "Which orders are past required_date?" - late order alert

## Sales Order Lines
Each order contains line items with:
- quantity_ordered: What customer requested
- quantity_shipped: What was actually sent
- unit_price: Price charged (may differ from product.unit_price)
